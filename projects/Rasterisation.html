Rasterisation

During my 3D algorithmics classes, I was asked to create a software running on java, to do Ray Tracing. There were only one rule, I needed to export the images in a weird format to not copy from other website. They gave me one class to export the images and one that manages float 3 dimensin vectors and I did the rest. Here are some examples of what my sofware was able to do. I made some bonus such as print the image in a window, made the Ray Tracing in parallel to generate images faster or change the float to double to have more precision. Unfortunatly, I didn't succed in making refraction, so you'll only see reflection.
