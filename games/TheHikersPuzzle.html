The Hiker's Puzzle

Context

This game was made during Ludum Dare 49 event, held between 2nd and 5th october 2021 online. Ludum Dare is an association known for hosting GameJams. It was my first gameJam outside of my educational background, and with friends from other sectors. My team participate with the Jam rules, which are explain here. But as to the rules, we only used what we created during the Jam. During this Jam our theme was : Unstable

Story

You are a hiker passing by a mountain when you see on the side of the road some slimes, afraid and lost. Due to your knowledge, you assume these are from the lab at the top of the mountain. Good thing you were heading there first, you decide to take the slimes with you to get them back where they belong. But Hiking a mountain this big is not something easy, and with passenger that are always moving, it's not gonna be an easy task. Will you be able to reach the top ?

Gameplay

The Hiker's puzzle is a puzzle game where you need to climb a mountain, watching your hunger, thirst and energy bars. During your climb, the slimes you carry are unstable, and will change form, which is going to stop you. You will need to solve a puzzle in order to get back on track. Also, you may encounter other slime on the track, which will make puzzles a bit harder. This is all we had think of, but the game does not implement all of those features, we weren't able to finished the game.
