Inspecteur Gadget

Context

During my last year of Master, I was welcomed by Smart-Tale to take part in the development of the Inspecteur Gadget - MAD Time party project. My internship lasted from 1 April 2023 to 30 September 2023, and it enabled me to validate my diploma.

Story

Metro City has fallen into the hands of the evil Dr. Claw and his M.A.D. agents. To save the city, Inspector Gadget must travel into the past using a time machine. Unfortunately, it breaks down and his ancestors find themselves teleported to the present. Explore Metro City and compete with your ancestors to collect the time machine’s missing parts.

Gameplay

This game is a party game with adventure elements. In singleplayer, explore Metroville and find the pieces of the the machine by winning against your ancestors in the various mini-games on offer. In multiplayer, discover the 3 game modes that let you challenge your friends.

Link to the game's page by the editor

My contribution to the game

I took part in various ports of the game. I helped optimise the game for Switch, allowing it to run on this console. I also set up the Playstation API for the Playstation 4 and 5 versions. On Playstation 5, I also set up the architecture to use the activities. Finally, I helped fix bugs across the game and consoles.
