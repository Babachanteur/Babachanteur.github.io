Hooman & Octo

Emergency failure in Poitiers

Context

This game was made during Gamejam Au Palais event, held between 4th and 6th november 2022 in Poitiers. It was the first GameJam where they mixed student from the European Image School EESI(École Européenne Supérieure de l'Image) and student from the University of Poitiers (mostly in IT). It made games more finished than in previous editions
