Hooman & Octo

Emergency failure in Poitiers

Context

This game was made during Gamejam au Palais event, held between 4th and 6th november 2022 in Poitiers. It was the first GameJam where they mixed student from the European Image School EESI(École Européenne Supérieure de l'Image) and student from the University of Poitiers (mostly in IT). It made games more finished than in previous editions. The theme for our edition was "Imagine Poitiers in 50, 100 or 500 years where the palace is still there, but humans are not the center of civilisation anymore".

Story

500 Years into the future, Earth is flooded. Global warming melted the poles, and now most of the land is under water. In this new environment a familiar species thrived: the Octopus. These animals have gained sentience and are now the dominant species on Earth, while the remains of Humanity are treated as lower class citizens, good only for creating insulated electrical circuits. In the City of Poitiers, the diplomatic capital of Octopus and Human Collaboration in France, disaster struck. The power plant of Saint-Pierre Cathedral has gone silent and the whole city is at risk of running out of electricity. Octopus are blaming the incident on Human population and a major diplomacy crisis is on our hands. To resolve this, the “Brigade d’Intervention Terrestre Et Sous-marine” calls for two of their best agents, Hooman the Octopus and Octo the Human, to investigate the power plant. Will they be able to do it?

Gameplay

This game is an asymetrical puzzle platformer where two players need to cooperate in order to advance in the game. They will control the two caracters you see at the last of this paragraph. They both have differents actions, and their actions change depending if they are underwater or not. If you want to try the demo, you can follow the link below to download it.
