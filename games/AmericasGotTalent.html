America's got talent

Context

This game was made during an event made by the association AEIF, held between 20th and 22nd november 2020 online. This association was the one of my faculty, so it was between IT student. For this gameJam the theme was : Virus

Story

You are an american who just hear on the news that a third containment is about to happen. And last time there was one, you weren't prepared. But that is going to change, because you are going to defend a supermarket against a lot of people ready to buy the goods you wanted for yourself. Will you be able to defend yourself ?

Gameplay

America's got talent is a tower-defense game where you need to defend the supermarket in center of the map. Multiples waves of ennemies will comme toward your way, and you will need to defeat them. In order to do that, you can move around with Z, Q, S, D, aim with the cursor and fire with Left Mouse Button. By defeating ennemies, you will gather money, that you can spend inside the garage of the store for upgrade, or on the places to put tower by pressing E.
